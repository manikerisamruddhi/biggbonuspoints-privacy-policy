Privacy Policy for BiggBonusPoints

Effective Date: 24 May 2025

At BiggBonusPoints, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information and permissions in our app.

1. Permissions Used

The BiggBonusPoints app requests access to your device’s camera. This permission is used solely for scanning QR codes as part of the app’s functionality for:

  • Customers earning and redeeming points at merchant locations
  • Merchants verifying customer interactions

No images, video, or personal data are stored, shared, or transmitted from your camera usage.

2. No Personal Data Collection

BiggBonusPoints does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All QR scanning is performed locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Access

We do not share your information with third parties. No data is transmitted outside the app as part of camera or QR code scanning operations.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
